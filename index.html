Gillian's Learner Credential Wallet

A place for storing and sharing digitally signed credentials and badges

The Learner Credential Wallet (LCW) is a mobile app developed by the MIT Digital Credentials Consortium for claiming, storing, and privately sharing digitally signed credentials and badges. Developed in 2020 through a grant from the U.S. Department of Education, the LCW is an open source, free-to-use reference implementation that demonstrates VC ecosystem functionality.
