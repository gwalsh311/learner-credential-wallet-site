Learner Credential Wallet Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy The LCW does not collect personal data. It also does not track how and when you use the wallet or where you share your public link. The public link is hosted at VerifierPlus, a website run by the DCC where anyone can verify your credential in real time at VerifierPlus.org


VerifierPlus hosts your credential data because mobile devices cannot host web pages directly. However, you control this link - you can delete it anytime by selecting “unshare” on the LCW share screen, which removes it from VerifierPlus as well. Like the LCW, VerifierPlus does not track where or with whom you share your links, but needs to collect personal information in order to perform its function.
You can review the Privacy Policy for VerifierPlus here.

The DCC believes you should have unrestricted access to your data and the ability to share it privately. Our software is designed with these principles in mind.
